A one-page introduction for school owners and principals

Your teachers mark attendance in one tap. Your fees arrive by UPI directly into the school's own account — no gateway, no convenience charges. And every notice, absence alert and fee receipt reaches parents where they already are: WhatsApp, in their own language.

✓  Attendance in one tap

Everyone starts present; teachers tap only the exceptions from their own phone. Absentees' parents get a WhatsApp alert by 9:45 am. You see every class's register status live on one board.

₹  Fees without friction

One button reminds every overdue family on WhatsApp with a UPI link carrying the exact balance. Receipts land in the same chat automatically. Dues dashboard shows who to call, oldest first.

◉  Parents reached, always

Notices, class diary and results updates in Hindi or English per family, with read-tracking. Families install nothing — ~98% of WhatsApp messages get read.

👥  A view for every role

Teachers see their class. The front desk sees money. You see everything. Everyone sees less and does more.
